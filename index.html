About

Hi, I'm Destinee! I specialize in marketing, with 2 years of experience as a designer and 2 years as a photo editor. I'm based in Orlando, FL!

I use bright colors, Cel shading, and bold shapes, which is why vector art is one of my best mediums. I am very open to creating other iterations of my drafts, and usually have many ideas that spontaneously branch off from each other.

I consider myself to be someone who thinks outside of the box; I try lots of options and ask others until I find what sticks. I thrive off of team feedback and love to bounce ideas off of other people. I want to bring a fun presence, while still keeping it productive.
