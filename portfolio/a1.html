Our Floor Plans

Please Contact the Office for Pricing

With an apartment home at The Adeline in Maitland, Florida, you have a multitude of luxuries right at your fingertips. Our impressive one, two and three-bedroom apartments showcase the perfect balance of sophistication and relaxation. Each one of our elegant, French-inspired homes have the finest interior features and designer finishes while select floor plans offer premium upgrades.

Our apartments include your choice of two unique kitchen finish packages: luxurious quartz or granite kitchen countertops with beautiful cabinetry in one of two designer-influenced palettes. Your contemporary open kitchen has refined subway tile backsplashes, and sleek, stainless-steel appliances to make your meal preparation and clean up convenient and easy. Elegant bathrooms come complete with tiled baths and luxurious vanities, and our two-bedroom apartment homes offer the upgrade of a walk-in shower.

Spacious walk-in closets will provide plenty of room for all your personal items and you’ll have the added convenience of a washer and dryer in select premium units. Every apartment home is equipped with an intrusion alarm and includes high speed internet to keep you and your family connected without interruption. For that special designer’s touch, there are ceiling fans and modern lighting throughout, as well as stylish wood plank flooring in your kitchen, bathroom(s) and living room. Plus, each home includes a charming private patio so you can enjoy the beauty of our community.

Be stylish and ready for anything. Just like your new home.
