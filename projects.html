Pegasus Penthouse Wall Wrap

January 2022

This project was one of my most exciting ones! I collaborated with one of my team members, Katie Drescher, to create this mural. We also worked with Coca-Cola and learned how to negotiate with them on certain design edits.

We worked in Illustrator to create the base proof that we then sent to Coca-Cola. We went back and forth on a couple of designs, but ultimately ended up with the one you see in the Student Union today.

Tiktok Tuesday Motion Graphic

May 2022

This motion graphic trio was made for a social media campaign, in which students send in TikToks according to a certain theme.

I used Illustrator to create the particle assets, Photoshop and Animate to create the hand, and After Effects to compose everything.

Pumpkin Palooza Team/Event Posters

September 2022

Pumpkin Palooza is a themed Halloween event that the Student Union puts on every Halloween. In 2022, for the 10th anniversary, the theme was Willy Wonka.

There was also a pumpkin carving contest, in which the aforementioned teams competed to make the best pumpkin. My job was to create some of the posters that would be at their tables.

I used Illustrator and Photoshop for these posters.

Market Day

Fall 2022/Spring 2023

Market Day is a weekly event outside the Student Union that's almost like a farmer's market. I used the head of the Citronaut mascot asset from my coworker Katie Drescher and illustrated the rest in a similar style.

I made these graphics in Illustrator.

Knights' Pantry Infographic

August 2022

Knights on the Mall

Mills 50 Project - UCF Adobe Creative Jam

April 2023

This video was submitted into a contest for the UCF Adobe Creative Jam. The goal was to spotlight a certain district or person important to Orlando, and our group chose the Mills 50 District. I worked with Katie Drescher and Henry Severe to film, brainstorm, and execute this project in a mere 4 days!

I used Illustrator to create the map assets and After Effects to animate.
